# Forkit - Community & Safety Guidelines (Global)
**Version 1.0**  
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-24

---

These guidelines explain how to use Forkit safely and responsibly. They are written plainly for ease of understanding and apply equally to all users (16+).

## 1. Our Purpose

Forkit is a community for sharing food-related knowledge in a way that prioritizes user safety. We encourage creativity, learning, and respectful discussion—never content that could cause harm.

## 2. Who Can Use Forkit

Forkit is for users aged 16 and older. Accurate age information is required.

## 3. Allowed Content

Share recipes, tips, stories, cultural context, ingredient information, and safe cooking techniques appropriate for public audiences (16+).

## 4. Prohibited Content (Zero-Tolerance)

Do not post:
- Instructions, procedures, or steps that could enable physical harm.
- Unsafe combinations, dosages, or handling methods.
- Content that can be pieced together into harmful real-world actions.
- Attempts to hide or hint at prohibited instructions.
- Requests that solicit harmful or dangerous guidance.

Intent (education, research, curiosity) does not make prohibited content acceptable.

## 5. Moderation and Reporting

We use automated tools and human reviewers to keep Forkit safe. Use the in-app report function to flag unsafe or suspicious content. Do not attempt to reproduce or distribute flagged content.

## 6. Enforcement

Consequences for violating rules include content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent ban. We may also notify legal authorities if required.

## 7. Respect and Community Behavior

Be respectful. Do not harass or deliberately mislead other users. Avoid misinformation that could put people at risk.

## 8. No Special Access or Exceptions

No one—researchers, professionals, or paid subscribers—has special permission to post prohibited content.

## 9. Updates

Guidelines may change. Continued use means you accept updates.

## 10. Contact

To report urgent safety concerns: safety@forkit.example
General support: support@forkit.example
