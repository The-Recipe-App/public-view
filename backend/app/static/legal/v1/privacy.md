# Forkit - Privacy Policy (Global)
**Version 1.0**  
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-24

---

## 1. Introduction

Forkit respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information in compliance with applicable global data-protection laws. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we process and protect it.

By using Forkit, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### a) Information You Provide
- Account details: email address, username, date of birth, profile information.
- Content you submit: recipes, comments, uploads, messages.

### b) Technical & Usage Data
- IP address and connection information.
- Device and browser information.
- Log data including timestamps, actions, and errors.
- Security and fraud-detection signals.

### c) Limited Third-Party Data
- Information obtained from third-party services you choose to connect (e.g., social login) in accordance with your permissions.

We do not collect highly sensitive personal data unless explicitly required and consented to for a specific feature.

---

## 3. How We Use Data

We process data for the following legitimate purposes:
- Account creation, authentication, and age verification.
- Content moderation and enforcement of safety rules.
- Security, fraud prevention, and abuse detection.
- Service improvement, analytics, and performance monitoring.
- Legal compliance, dispute resolution, and law-enforcement cooperation.

We will not sell personal data.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

Depending on your jurisdiction, processing is based on:
- Consent you provide.
- Performance of the contract to provide the Service.
- Legitimate interests (security and safety).
- Legal obligations requiring processing or disclosure.

We may share personal data with:
